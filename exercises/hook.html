Hook Head Lighthouse

Where history meets the sea

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world and explore its storied past with expert guides.

Climb the ancient tower, learn about its medieval origins, and enjoy panoramic views from the top.

Coastal Walks & Wildlife

Wander along dramatic cliffs and spot seals, dolphins, and seabirds in their natural habitat.

Interpretive trails and viewing points make it a perfect outing for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Visitor Centre & Café

Relax in the welcoming visitor centre with interactive exhibits and local maritime history displays.

Enjoy homemade treats and fresh coffee at the café while soaking in the sea breeze and stunning views.
